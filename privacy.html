Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

holddr (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a mobile application that helps you manage impulse spending and build healthier money habits. This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Summary

holddr stores your data on your device only. We do not operate user accounts, cloud sync, or backend servers for the current version of the app. Your progress, journal entries, chat with Holddr Therapy, and settings stay on your phone unless you choose to share something outside the app (for example, by email).

Information stored on your device

The app may store locally, using your device’s storage (AsyncStorage), including:

  • Onboarding and quiz answers
  • Recovery streak and relapse logs
  • Vault / spending journal entries
  • Reasons for change and profile display name
  • Dashboard preferences and content-blocker settings
  • Community posts, clan membership, and friend invite codes (local demo data)
  • Messages you exchange with Holddr Therapy during a session (cleared when you leave that screen)

Holddr Therapy (AI coach)

Holddr Therapy provides automated, rule-based replies on your device. Messages are not sent to our servers and are not used to train external AI models. Conversations are cleared when you exit the therapy screen to protect your privacy.

Holddr Therapy is not a licensed therapist, doctor, or financial advisor. Do not rely on it for medical, mental health, or financial decisions.

What we do not collect

  • No account registration or password in the current version
  • No payment processing in the current version
  • No advertising identifiers used by us for tracking
  • No sale of personal data to third parties

Third-party services

The app is built with Expo / React Native. App stores (Apple App Store, Google Play) may collect standard download and device information according to their own policies when you install or update the app. We do not control those services.

Children

holddr is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention and deletion

Data remains on your device until you delete it within the app (where available), use “Log out” to return to onboarding, or uninstall the app. Uninstalling holddr removes locally stored app data from your device.

Security

Because data stays on your device, you are responsible for keeping your device secure (passcode, biometrics, backups). We cannot recover your data if you lose your device or uninstall the app.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes (for example, if we add accounts or cloud sync). We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
